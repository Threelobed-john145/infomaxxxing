## 🔧 Basic troubleshooting

### App does not open

### Text looks cut off

- Resize the window
- Maximize the app
- Change your display scaling in Windows
- Restart the app after changing the window size

### Scroll feels jumpy

- Use the mouse wheel in smaller steps
- Try the touchpad scroll gesture
- Close other apps that may slow your PC
